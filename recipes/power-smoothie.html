Power Smoothie

Description

Power smoothies are a great start to any day! Even if you get out on the wrong side of the bed, you can still salvage the day with this incredibly easy recipe! Just make to to leave the washing up soaking in the sink when you leave for work.

Ingredients

  • 1 banana
  • 1 cup mixed berries (fresh or frozen)
  • 1 cup milk (or almond/oat milk)
  • 1 tbsp honey or maple syrup (optional)
  • A few ice cubes (optional)

Steps

  1. Toss everything into a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth.
  3. Pour into that to-go cup and enjoy!
